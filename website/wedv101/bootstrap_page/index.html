Kabunga Holdings Pty Ltd

Kabunga Coffee is a held item introduced in Loomian Legacy - Veils of Shadow that can be given to any Loomian. It is obtained in a café with the same name at Cheshma Town. More cups of Kabunga Coffee can be bought in Colosseum Marketplace.

What We Do

According to a customer in Kabunga Coffee, Cheshma Town, Kabunga makes this item by dancing.

Kabunga Coffee is the replacement of Sawsbuck Coffee from the prequel game Pokémon Brick Bronze.Similarly to Sawsbuck Coffee, additional cups of Kabunga Coffee is not available in the café; instead, the player has to buy Kabunga Coffee in Colosseum Marketplace if he or she needs more.
